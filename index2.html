Dental Services

At Dental Care, we've made sure that no matter what your dental needs are, we can help. We have a large list of dental care services at our Chicago, IL office that are designed to whiten, brighten, straighten, and strengthen your beautiful smile.

Dental Implants

Dental implants are strong, durable, and natural-looking teeth replacements that can last for 20 years or more with the proper care. We offer dental implants for only $1500!

Dentures

Dentures are essentially false teeth that are custom made to fit your mouth perfectly. They can be used to replace a single tooth, multiple teeth, or even a full set of teeth.

Invisalign速

Invisalign速 is clear, custom, plastic aligners created to align a patient's teeth over the span of a few months or years. This treatment is known to be a popular and effective solution!

BOTOX速 Dentistry

BOTOX速 dentistry is the use of botulinum toxin to temporarily reduce or eliminate wrinkles in the facial area. It can also be used to treat TMJ disorders and teeth grinding.

Emergency Services

There are a few different types of dental emergencies that require different types of treatment. If you have a tooth that has been knocked out or a toothache, come see us!

Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic dentistry is about improving the function of your teeth in a way that also leaves your pearly whites looking bright and beautiful. From whitening to veneers, we do it all!
